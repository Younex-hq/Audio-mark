# Audio-mark

## Reason Behinde this Webapp
NotebookLM allows you to generate audio overviews, but it lacks a few key features. For instance, you cannot mark specific points in the audio or take general notes while listening. This web application was created to solve that gap by enabling users to annotate, save, and later resume their notes directly from the audio.

## How to Use
1. Download the Audio Overview: Begin by downloading your audio overview from NotebookLM.
2. Upload the Audio: Upload the audio file to the web app.
3. Listen and Annotate: Play the audio and add notes or markers as you listen.
4. Export Your Annotations: Export your notes for future reference.
5. Resume Easily Next Time: When you return, simply upload the audio and import your saved notes to continue from exactly where you left off
